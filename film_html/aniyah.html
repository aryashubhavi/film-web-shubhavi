ANIYAH



❮
❯


-Short Narrative Fiction-

In the drama short ANIYAH, a mother and her little daughter deal with the struggle of living apart from their husband and dad after he moves to the city in search of a job. ANIYAH is a short film about cherished motherhood, family and sacrifices.

Director, Writer, Cinematographer - Shubhavi Arya
Editor - Saatvik Arya, Shubhavi Arya
Makeup and Hair Designer - Kelly Handevidt

Starring:
Iris Seifert, Melissa Paakh, Timothy Herkenhoff,
Elizabeth Couture, Kyli Nault, Leah James,
Kelly Handevidt, Leakina Jones, Darion Jones
